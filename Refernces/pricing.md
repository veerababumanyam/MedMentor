### 12.2 Competitive pricing benchmarks (captured 2026-02-19; subject to change)
Note: some competitor pricing pages are consent-gated or blocked in this environment (e.g., AMBOSS, parts of UWorld medical), so this table captures only what was publicly retrievable.

| Tool | What students buy it for | Published pricing (observed) | Source |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Sketchy | Visual memory + cases/QBank bundle | All-access: $349.99/6 mo ($58.33/mo), $449.99/12 mo ($37.49/mo), $649.99/24 mo ($27.08/mo). Cases-only: $19.99/mo or $119.99/12 mo ($9.99/mo). | https://www.sketchy.com/ |
| Osmosis (MD/DO/PA/NP) | All-in-one schedule + videos + questions + flashcards | Trial €0/7 days. €44/mo. €229/yr (≈€19/mo). €369/2 yrs (≈€15/mo). | https://www.osmosis.org/plans/md/ (also /do, /pa, /np) |
| Osmosis (RN) | Nursing-focused plan | Trial €0/7 days. €29/mo. €74/6 mo (≈€12/mo). €129/yr (≈€11/mo). | https://www.osmosis.org/plans/rn/ |
| RemNote | Notes + spaced repetition + AI features | Free €0. Pro €8/mo (€96 billed yearly). Pro with AI €18/mo (€216 billed yearly). | https://www.remnote.com/pricing |
| Anki | Spaced repetition baseline | Desktop is free; iOS companion app is a one-time purchase. | https://apps.ankiweb.net/ and https://apps.apple.com/us/app/ankimobile-flashcards/id373493387 |
| AnkiMobile (iOS) | Official iOS app | $24.99 one-time purchase (US App Store listing). | https://apps.apple.com/us/app/ankimobile-flashcards/id373493387 |
| AnkiHub | Community deck collaboration + AI tools | Free $0. Core $6/mo. Premium $10/mo. Lifetime $450. | https://www.ankihub.net/ |
